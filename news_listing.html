Fire force rescues family after man raises death threat!

Kochi: Fire Force officials rescued a family after a man locked his wife and son in their houseand thretened to kill himself and his family by opening the cooking gas cylinder and setting it fire. The incident happened at Maradu on Wednesday. Police said that it was a 45-year-old man who was staying at a rented house who raised the threat. Tripunithura fire force officials were alerted about the incident, and they rushed to the spot. A team under Tripunithara fire station officer K Manoharan and assistant fire station officer Vinu Raj tried to calm him down even as they kept fire tending measures ready to react in case he teakes the extreme step.
